Enroll in a Course

A course is a 10 week long set of classes.

Course Information

  • Weekday classes meet twice a week for 2 hours each session. Weekend classes meet once a week for a total of 4 1/2 hours: Math for 2 hours, then a 30 minute break, followed by Verbal for 2 hours. Each SAT class is the same, so attending multiple sites is not beneficial. We ask that you commit to attending at least 80% of the classroom sessions, so please enroll only if you are serious about taking the course.
  • Our courses are geared toward the May or June exam. Please do not enroll in a 10 week course if you plan to take the March exam. Instead, check out our workshops!
  • Students are free to attend any of our sites. Once you have registered, you are enrolled in the course! We will send a confirmation email. You are welcome to attend a class, even if the first few meetings have already occurred. We prefer, however, that you enroll online before beginning to attend!

Enroll in a Workshop

A workshop is a one day course.

Workshop Information

  • We offer free Saturday workshops at the YMCA PG&E Teen Center (2111 Martin Luther King Jr Way, Berkeley). Registration is open to any interested high school students, including those who attend our weekly classes elsewhere. These workshops are meant to be supplementary to our weekly courses and are not replacements for our normal curriculum.
  • Please note that while these workshops are free-of-charge, prior online registration is recommended for a guaranteed spot. Bring note-taking materials. You are welcome to register for as many workshops as you want.
