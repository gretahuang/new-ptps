Enroll in a Course

A course is a 10 week long set of classes.

Course Information

  • Weekday classes meet twice a week for 2 hours each session. Weekend classes meet once a week for a total of 4 1/2 hours: Math for 2 hours, then a 30 minute break, followed by Verbal for 2 hours. Each SAT class has the same standardized curriculum, so students only need to sign up for one site. We urge parents and students to commit to attending at least 80% of classes so that teachers have ample time to work with students to best prepare them for the SAT.
  • Our courses are 10 weeks long. We ask that students do not enroll in our courses if they plan to take the SAT before the course is over. Instead, check out our workshops!
  • Students are welcome to sign up for any of our sites. Once you have filled out the online form, you will receive an email to confirm your enrollment.

Enroll in a Workshop

A workshop is a one day course.

Workshop Information

  • We offer free Saturday workshops at the YMCA PG&E Teen Center (2111 Martin Luther King Jr Way, Berkeley). Registration is open to any interested high school students, including those who attend our weekly classes elsewhere. These workshops are meant to be supplementary to our weekly courses and are not replacements for our normal curriculum.
  • Please note that while these workshops are free-of-charge, prior online registration is recommended for a guaranteed spot. Bring note-taking materials. You are welcome to register for as many workshops as you want.
